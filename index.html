🏏 Cricket Basics - Shuru Karte Hain!

1. Cricket Kya Hai?
Cricket ek team sport hai jisme 11-11 players hote hain. Ek team batting karti hai aur dusri bowling. Goal hai: Jitne zyada runs banao aur opponent ko kam runs par out karo!
2. Basic Equipment
Bat: Runs banane ke liye 🏏
Ball: Red (Test), White (ODI/T20) ⚪
Stumps: 3 wickets jo defend karni hoti hain
Pads & Gloves: Safety ke liye 🧤
3. Basic Rules
Runs: Ball ko hit karke wickets ke beech bhaago
Boundary: 4 runs (ground touch karke) ya 6 runs (direct)
Out: Bowled, Caught, LBW, Run out, Stumped
Overs: 6 balls = 1 over
4. Match Formats
Test Cricket: 5 days, unlimited overs (traditional)
ODI: 50 overs per team (One Day International)
T20: 20 overs per team (Fast & exciting!) ⚡

🏏 Batting Skills - Runs Banao!

1. Batting Stance
Grip: Bat ko comfortably pakdo, V-shape banao hands se
Stance: Sideways khade raho, knees thode bend
Eyes: Ball par focus rakho, head still rakho
Balance: Weight equally distribute karo dono legs par
2. Basic Shots
Straight Drive: Seedha maro, elegant shot! 🎯
Cover Drive: Side mein maro, beautiful shot
Pull Shot: Short ball ko leg side mein
Cut Shot: Short ball ko off side mein
Sweep: Spin bowling ke against
3. Batting Tips for Beginners
✅ Pehle defense seekho, phir attack
✅ Ball ko dekhte raho, bat nahi
✅ Timing > Power (force mat lagao)
✅ Practice makes perfect!
✅ Patience rakho, har ball ko marne ki zarurat nahi
4. Common Mistakes to Avoid
❌ Ball se dar mat lagao
❌ Pehli ball se sixer mat marne ki koshish karo
❌ Eyes band mat karo while hitting
❌ Overconfident mat bano

⚡ Bowling Guide - Wickets Lo!

1. Bowling Basics
Run-up: Rhythm ke saath daudna
Action: Smooth aur consistent
Release: Ball ko sahi time par chhodo
Follow-through: Action complete karo
2. Types of Bowling
Fast Bowling: Speed aur bounce (140+ kmph) 🔥
Medium Pace: Control aur swing (120-135 kmph)
Spin Bowling: Ball ko ghumao, batsman ko confuse karo 🌀
Off-spin: Right-hander ke liye
Leg-spin: Tricky aur difficult
3. Bowling Variations
Yorker: Batsman ke feet par 🎯
Bouncer: Short ball, head ke paas
Slower Ball: Speed kam karke batsman ko fool karo
Swing: Ball ko hawa mein move karao
4. Bowling Tips
✅ Line aur length consistent rakho
✅ Batsman ki weakness dhundo
✅ Variations use karo
✅ Fitness important hai
✅ Captain ki field setting follow karo

🤾‍♀️ Fielding Tips - Catches Win Matches!

1. Fielding Basics
Ready Position: Alert raho, knees bend
Eyes on Ball: Ball ko track karo
Quick Reflexes: Fast react karo
Strong Throw: Accurate aur powerful
2. Catching Techniques
High Catch: Hands upar, fingers up 👆
Low Catch: Hands neeche, fingers down 👇
Diving Catch: Full commitment! 🤸‍♀️
Tip: Soft hands rakho, ball ko cushion karo
3. Fielding Positions
Slip: Wicket-keeper ke paas
Point: Batsman ke side
Cover: Off side mein
Mid-wicket: Leg side mein
Long-on/Long-off: Boundary par
Fine Leg: Behind wicket-keeper
4. Fielding Tips
✅ Always stay alert aur ready
✅ Back up throws - teammates ki help karo
✅ Communicate loudly - "Mine!" bolo
✅ Save runs - diving stops karo
✅ Positive energy maintain karo
