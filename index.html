📚 Cricket Basics - Shuru Karte Hain!

Sabse pehle basics strong karo!

🏏 Cricket Kya Hai?

Cricket ek team sport hai jisme 11-11 players hote hain. Ek team batting karti hai aur dusri bowling. Goal hai: Jitne zyada runs banao aur opponent ko kam runs par out karo!

Fun Fact: Cricket ka naam "cricket" insect se nahi aaya! Ye Old French word "criquet" se aaya hai jiska matlab hai "stick" 🏏

🎯 Basic Equipment

🏏 Bat: Runs banane ke liye - willow wood se banta hai

⚪ Ball: Red (Test), White (ODI/T20) - leather ka hota hai

🎯 Stumps: 3 wickets jo defend karni hoti hain

🧤 Pads & Gloves: Safety ke liye - bahut important!

⛑️ Helmet: Head protection - fast bowling ke against

📖 Basic Rules - Simple Mein!

🏃 Runs: Ball ko hit karke wickets ke beech bhaago

4️⃣ Boundary: 4 runs (ground touch karke) ya 6 runs (direct)

❌ Out: Bowled, Caught, LBW, Run out, Stumped

⚡ Overs: 6 balls = 1 over

🏆 Win: Zyada runs banao = Jeet!

⏱️ Match Formats

🏛️ Test Cricket: 5 days, unlimited overs (traditional & longest)

🌍 ODI: 50 overs per team (One Day International)

⚡ T20: 20 overs per team (Fast & exciting! - Sabse popular)

💡 Tip: Beginners ke liye T20 sabse easy hai samajhne mein!

🏏 Batting Masterclass - Runs Banao!

Sabhi shots aur techniques seekho!

🎯 Perfect Batting Stance

✋ Grip: Bat ko comfortably pakdo, V-shape banao hands se

🧍 Stance: Sideways khade raho, knees thode bend

👀 Eyes: Ball par focus rakho, head still rakho

⚖️ Balance: Weight equally distribute karo dono legs par

💡 Pro Tip: Relaxed raho, tense mat ho!

⚡ Classic Shots - Master Karo!

🎯 Straight Drive

Sabse elegant shot! Ball ko seedha maro. Full length ball ke liye perfect.

⭐ Difficulty: Medium

🌟 Cover Drive

Beautiful shot! Ball ko off side mein through covers. Timing is key!

⭐ Difficulty: Medium

💪 Pull Shot

Power shot! Short ball ko leg side mein. Confidence chahiye!

⭐ Difficulty: Hard

⚡ Cut Shot

Quick shot! Short ball ko off side mein. Fast reflexes!

⭐ Difficulty: Medium

🔄 Sweep Shot

Spin bowling ke against best! Ball ko leg side mein sweep karo.

⭐ Difficulty: Medium

🚁 Helicopter Shot

MS Dhoni special! Wrist work se ball ko ghuma ke maro. Advanced!

⭐ Difficulty: Expert

✅ Batting Tips for Beginners

✅ Pehle defense seekho, phir attack - Foundation strong rakho!

✅ Ball ko dekhte raho, bat nahi - Eyes on the ball!

✅ Timing > Power - Force mat lagao, timing se khelo

✅ Practice makes perfect - Daily practice karo!

✅ Patience rakho - Har ball ko marne ki zarurat nahi

✅ Footwork important hai - Feet move karo ball ke saath

⚡ Bowling Academy - Wickets Lo!

Batsman ko out karna seekho!

🎯 Bowling Basics

🏃 Run-up: Rhythm ke saath daudna - Consistent rakho

🎬 Action: Smooth aur repeatable - Practice se perfect hoga

🎯 Release: Ball ko sahi time par chhodo - Timing crucial hai

🔄 Follow-through: Action complete karo - Energy transfer ke liye

📏 Line & Length: Consistent rakho - Batsman ko pressure do

🔥 Types of Bowling

🔥 Fast Bowling

Speed aur bounce! 140+ kmph. Pace se batsman ko intimidate karo!

Famous: Jasprit Bumrah, Brett Lee

💨 Medium Pace

Control aur swing! 120-135 kmph. Accuracy important hai!

Famous: Bhuvneshwar Kumar

🌀 Spin Bowling

Ball ko ghumao! Batsman ko confuse karo. Variations se wickets lo!

Famous: Ravichandran Ashwin, Shane Warne

🎯 Bowling Variations - Batsman Ko Fool Karo!

🎯 Yorker: Batsman ke feet par - Sabse difficult ball to play!

⚡ Bouncer: Short ball, head ke paas - Intimidation factor!

🐌 Slower Ball: Speed kam karke batsman ko fool karo

🌊 Swing: Ball ko hawa mein move karao - In-swing ya out-swing

🔄 Off-spin: Ball right-hander ke liye turn karta hai

🌀 Leg-spin: Opposite direction - Tricky aur difficult!

🎭 Googly: Leg-spinner ka secret weapon - Opposite turn!
